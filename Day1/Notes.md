# CYBER SECURITY
- Cyber security is the practice of protecting computer, networks, data from attacks.
  
**1.CIA Triad**

- Confidentiality:
    Data should only be accessed to authorized ppl.
    Example: Password.
- Integrity:
  Data must remain accurate and unchanged unless modified by the user.
-  Availability:
  Systems and data must be available when needed by authorized person.
  Ex: Backup systems.

**2.Types of Cyber Threats:**

- Phishing:Using fake emails to steal data.
- Malware:It is a combo of malicious software which designed to damage or access your system.
- Ransomware:Malware that locks or encrypts your files and demand money to unlock them.
- Dos attack:
  Attackers flood a website or server with heavy traffic.
  Ex:Amazon sale Traffics
- Inside Threats:
   Threat from employee, contractors, or internal people.
   Ex:An employee who resigned copies customer data to a pen drive and sells it to competitiors.
